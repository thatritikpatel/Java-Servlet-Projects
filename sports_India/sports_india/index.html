Inarguably, there is no dearth of sports talent in India, but we need to know how to convert these yet-to-be-discovered players into world-class sportspersons. This athletic competition was conceptualised to inculcate a spirit of competition and sportsmanship in children and to push them to challenge themselves and surpass their limitations. It also provides an opportunity for NGOs to interact with each other. For instance, in Mumbai over 600 children from different NGOs participate in the Inter NGO Sports Meet each year. Organised by Secure Giving, the event has the Athletics Federation of India as its technical partner in Mumbai and Delhi , the Karnataka Athletics Association as its partner in Bangalore as well as other athletic federations in the other cities.
